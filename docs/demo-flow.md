# GRIDSENTINEL-X Ω — Demo Flow Script

**Total Demo Time:** 3-4 minutes

### 1. The Hook (30s)
*Open on National Command Center*
"Judges, welcome to GRIDSENTINEL-X Omega. Traditional grids monitor failures. This is a cognitive grid that thinks, predicts, and autonomously defends itself. What you're seeing is a real-time live digital twin of 4 major Indian cities."

### 2. The Black Swan (60s)
*Switch to Black Swan Simulator*
"Let's test it with something extreme. A Category 4 Cyclone hits Chennai coast. Watch the system."
*Launch Scenario*
- Point out the cascading failures T+30s, T+60s.
- Highlight the **Economic Impact**: "Notice the ₹430 Crore potential loss."
- Show the **Autonomous Healing**: "But within seconds, our Self-Healing AI reroutes power and sheds non-critical load, preventing a total collapse."

### 3. The Agent Debate Chamber (60s)
*Switch to Agent Debate Room*
"How did it make that decision? We don't use a black box. We use an Agent Debate Chamber."
*Trigger Analysis*
"Watch as 12 specialized AI agents—Grid Optimizer, Economic Intelligence, Failure Prediction—debate the best course of action. The Optimizer suggests load shedding, but Economic Intelligence argues for targeted migration to save ₹18 Crores. The Mission Commander makes the final, explainable decision."

### 4. The Future Grid Designer (45s)
*Switch to Future Grid Designer*
"Finally, it's not just about defense; it's about the future. I type: 'Design Mumbai's grid for 2035 with 80% renewables'."
*Generate Blueprint*
"The AI forecasts demand, calculates the optimal solar/wind/battery mix, prices the infrastructure in Crores, and gives us a complete strategic blueprint."

### 5. The Close (15s)
"GRIDSENTINEL-X Omega: The world's first autonomous energy defense platform. Thank you."
